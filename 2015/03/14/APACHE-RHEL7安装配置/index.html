二．虚拟主机配置

    1. 查看虚拟主机配置文件：
      1.1 rpm -ql httpd | grep httpd-vhost
      输出如下：

1.2 拷贝配置文件到：/etc/httpd/conf.d （Apache子配置文件存放路径）
cp /usr/share/doc/httpd-2.4.6/httpd-vhosts.conf /etc/httpd/conf.d
1.4 修改/etc/hosts文件：vi /etc/hosts
127.0.0.1 localhost localhost.localdomain localhost4 localhost4.localdomain4
::1 localhost localhost.localdomain localhost6 localhost6.localdomain6
192.168.232.131 www.openlab.com www.redhat.com
注：192.168.232.131为本机IP
1.3 修改配置文件：vim /etc/httpd/conf.d/httpd-vhosts.conf

ServerName 192.168.232.131


ServerAdmin webmaster@openlab.com
DocumentRoot “/var/www/openlab”
ServerName www.openlab.com
ErrorLog “/var/log/httpd/openlab.com-error_log”
CustomLog “/var/log/httpd/openlab.com-access_log” common
ServerAdmin webmaster@redhat.com
DocumentRoot “/var/www/redhat”
ServerName www.RedHat.com
ErrorLog “/var/log/httpd/redhat.com-error_log”
CustomLog “/var/log/httpd/redhat.com-access_log” common

    1. 修改主配置文件：vi /etc/httpd/conf/httpd.conf
      AllowOverride All

    1. 在/var/www/html新建.htaccess文件： touch .htaccess

    1. 编辑.htaccess文件：
      AuthName “Please input your name and password”
      AuthType basic
      AuthUserFile /var/www/html/user.txt
      require valid-user

    1. 在/var/www/html新建user.txt文件
    1. 运行命令：htpasswd –b /var/www/html/use.txt hanhan 12345
    1. 重启服务
